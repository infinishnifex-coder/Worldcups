FAQ

QUESTIONS?
ANSWERED

What ad formats do you accept?
We accept high-quality JPEG images and high-quality video files (MP4 or MOV recommended). Our team will confirm exact specs for your booked slot duration after payment is received.
When will my ad air?
Your ad airs the day after the next scheduled World Cup 2026 match day, once your on-chain payment is confirmed and your creative has been received and approved.
How do I pay?
Payment is made via Ethereum (ETH) on the Ethereum Mainnet, either through MetaMask or by sending ETH manually to the address provided. We confirm payment automatically once it's detected on-chain.
What if a match is postponed or cancelled?
If the match day associated with your booking is postponed, your ad will automatically be rescheduled to the day after the next available match day at no extra cost.
Can I get a refund?
Refund eligibility depends on timing and whether your creative has been submitted. Cancellations are accepted within 24 hours of payment if creative hasn't been submitted. See our Terms below or contact us directly.
Terms & Refund Policy

THE FINE PRINT

1. Booking & Payment

All ad slots are confirmed only once payment in ETH is verified on the Ethereum blockchain. Bookings are first-come, first-served. Until on-chain confirmation is received, a slot is not guaranteed.

2. Ad Creative

Clients must submit creative assets within 48 hours of payment confirmation. We reserve the right to reject content that is unlawful, offensive, or does not meet broadcast standards. If creative is not received in time, the slot may be forfeited without refund.

3. Match Postponement or Cancellation

If the match day associated with your booking is postponed, your ad will be automatically rescheduled to air the day after the next available match day at no additional cost. If the tournament is cancelled entirely, clients will be offered a full refund to the originating wallet address.

4. Cancellations & Refunds

Clients may request a cancellation and refund within 24 hours of payment confirmation, provided creative has not yet been submitted or scheduled. After this window, bookings are non-refundable. Refunds are issued in ETH to the original wallet address.

5. Restricted Content

We do not accept advertisements promoting illegal goods or services, gambling where prohibited, tobacco, or content violating broadcast standards. We reserve the right to decline any booking at our discretion.

6. Contact

For any questions, contact us at Romygai@outlook.com.
